# Content Tone

Your copy should consistently communicate:

* Outcome-focused: emphasize business growth, customer acquisition, and credibility rather than just design.
* Professional but approachable: avoid excessive technical jargon when speaking to business owners.
* Clear and concise: short paragraphs, strong headings, and obvious calls to action.
* Confident without exaggeration: let your work and process build trust.
