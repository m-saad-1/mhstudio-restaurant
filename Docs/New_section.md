I recommend **not** calling it **AI Chatbot**. Most businesses already know what a chatbot is, and the term can sound generic. Since you're targeting restaurants, **AI Receptionist** is more outcome-focused and immediately conveys business value.

Here are a few strong section title options:

* **AI Receptionist** ⭐ *(Recommended)*
* **Meet Your AI Receptionist**
* **24/7 AI Receptionist**
* **Always-On AI Assistant**
* **Restaurant AI Assistant**
* **Smart Customer Assistant**
